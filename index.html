Who We Are

Built for Nigeria's Most Ambitious Students

P.Kay Digital Enterprise Ltd was born from a single conviction: that no talented Nigerian student should lose their university spot to registration errors, poor preparation, or lack of access to quality tools. We are headquartered in Port Harcourt, Rivers State, with nationwide digital reach.

Our operation is a seamless vertical integration of two industry-defining divisions. KB's JAMB Services is the student-facing powerhouse — the trusted name thousands of candidates and parents call first when JAMB season arrives. We handle JAMB registration, data corrections, institutional Post-UTME screening runs, and every complex portal logistics challenge that leaves candidates panicking.

PrintKay's Tech is our engineering backbone — the invisible engine that powers the most sophisticated CBT simulation platform in Nigeria. Built on high-performance frontend architecture with precision-designed algorithms, our platform mirrors the exact UI of JAMB and Post-UTME portals, eliminates exam anxiety, and trains candidates to perform at their absolute peak.

Together, these divisions create an unstoppable ecosystem that has produced a track record no competitor can match: 1,200+ flawless registrations, 15,000+ completed mock sessions, and an 88% success rate of students scoring 250 and above. We are not a cyber café. We are a digital institution.
